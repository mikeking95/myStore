20Minute Honey Garlic Shrimp

Ingredients

  • 1/3 Cup Honey
  • 1/4 Cup Soy Sauce
  • 1 Tblspoon jarred minced garlic (2 Fresh, minced)
  • 1lb Medium uncooked Shrimp, peeled/deveined
  • 2 Tblspoon Olive Oil
  • Optional: Green Onion, Chopped (Garnish)
